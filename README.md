# 🎤 Vocalo

### Find the music your voice was made for.

Vocalo is a web application that helps users discover which music genres and songs best match their voice.

By recording a few vocal samples, users receive an analysis of their vocal range, voice type, and singing characteristics. Vocalo then recommends suitable genres, artists, and karaoke songs that fit their natural voice.

---

## Overview

Many people enjoy singing but struggle to find songs that fit their vocal range. They often choose songs that are too high, too low, or uncomfortable to perform.

Vocalo solves this problem by analyzing a user's voice and providing personalized recommendations based on their natural singing ability.

The experience is designed to be simple, fun, and music-focused, helping users better understand their voice and discover songs they can confidently sing.

---

## Features

### Vocal Range Detection

Analyze a user's lowest and highest comfortable notes to determine their singing range.

### Voice Type Classification

Identify common voice categories such as:

* Bass
* Baritone
* Tenor
* Alto
* Mezzo-Soprano
* Soprano

### Genre Recommendations

Recommend music genres that naturally complement the user's vocal characteristics.

Examples include:

* Pop
* Indie Pop
* Acoustic
* Rock
* R&B
* Country
* Folk

### Karaoke Song Suggestions

Provide song recommendations that match the user's range and vocal profile.

Each recommendation includes:

* Song title
* Artist
* Difficulty level
* Match score

### Artist Matching

Suggest artists whose vocal characteristics align with the user's voice.

### Shareable Results

Generate personalized result cards that users can share on social media.

---

## User Experience

1. User starts a vocal test.
2. User records their lowest comfortable note.
3. User records their highest comfortable note.
4. User optionally sings a short sample.
5. Vocalo analyzes the recording.
6. User receives their vocal profile.
7. Personalized genres, artists, and karaoke songs are recommended.

## Design Vision

Vocalo is inspired by modern music platforms such as Spotify, Apple Music, and Shazam.

The visual identity focuses on:

* Animated sound waves
* Music-inspired interactions
* Dark immersive backgrounds
* Neon gradients
* Audio visualizers
* Glassmorphism effects
* Modern typography

The goal is to create an experience that feels like a combination of a music streaming platform and a personal vocal coach.
